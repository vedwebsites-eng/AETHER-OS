## 🧠 What is AetherOS?

AetherOS is a **cyberpunk-themed, gamified self-improvement operating system**. It replaces Notion, Done, Day One, Superhuman, and YouTube — with one integrated platform where everything talks to everything.

Your mood updates your life balance. Your habits feed your Wheel of Life. Your AI coach reads all your data and gives advice that's actually specific to **you** — not generic productivity tips.

```
Notion + Done + Day One + Superhuman + YouTube coach = AetherOS
```

**Free forever. No credit card. No paywall on core features.**

### 6 Core Modules

---

### 🖥 CORE_COMMAND — Dashboard

![Dashboard](dashboard.png)

Your mission control. The moment you open AetherOS, you see everything at once:

- Profile card with level, XP bar, and streak counter
- Life balance overview (Wheel of Life mini-view)
- Core metrics — XP, tasks, streak, journal count, life alignment
- Quick Override panel — upcoming tasks, debrief alerts
- Real-time activity feed

---

### ⚡ DAILY_WORK — Tasks + Habits + Timetable

![Daily Work](dailywork.png)

The engine room. Where you actually get things done:

- **TASK_QUEUE** — Priority-based tasks (Low/Medium/High/Critical) with XP rewards, challenge mode, speed bonuses, and a **24-hour countdown timer** on every task
- **ROUTINE_MATRIX** — Daily habit checkboxes with 52-week GitHub-style heatmap, per-habit streaks, category XP multipliers
- **TEMPORAL_SYNC** — AI-powered timetable with month/week/day views, Pomodoro timer (25/5 or 50/10), and AI scheduler that builds your entire day
- Integrated sidebar for fast task + habit creation

---

### 📝 REFLECT — Journal + Insights

![Journal](journal.png)

Not just a journal. A neural archive:

- **Rich text editor** (TipTap) with bold, italic, lists, links, colors
- **Energy Index** — rate your energy (Drained / Low / OK / High / Peak)
- **Neural Tags** — #stressed, #energetic, #productive, #grateful and more
- **Protocol Reflection Prompts** — 50 rotating prompts with +25 XP bonus
- **Archive** — collapsible history cards showing content, mood, word count, and reflection prompt used
- **Insights** — mood stability charts, word density trends, temporal frequency analysis
- Word count XP scaling — the more you write, the more you earn

---

### 📈 GROW — Wheel of Life + Stats + Achievements

Track 8 life areas on a fully customizable radar chart:

- **LIFE_SYNC** — Wheel of Life with 8 axes (Gym, Diet, Love, Studies, Finance, Sleep, Social, Mental Health). Customizable — add or remove axes. Auto-updates from habits and journal mood.
- **NEURAL_EVOLUTION** — Month-over-month stats comparison, 30 achievements across 4 categories (Milestones, Streaks, Skills, Hidden)
- **Weekly Snapshots** — track your life balance week by week
- **Share** — export your Wheel of Life as a shareable PNG card

---

### 🧠 AETHER_COACH — AI Life Coach

Not a generic chatbot. A coach that actually knows you:

- Reads your **level, XP, streak, achievements**
- Reads your **last 7 journal entries** and mood trends
- Reads your **current Wheel of Life scores**
- Reads your **task completion rate** (this week vs last)
- Reads your **habit streaks** and consistency
- Gives advice specific to YOUR data — not generic productivity tips
- Persistent conversation history (last 50 messages)
- Suggested quick prompts for faster interactions

## 🎮 Gamification Engine

AetherOS is gamified from the ground up. Everything gives you XP.

| Source | XP | Daily Cap |
|--------|-----|-----------|
| Tasks | Priority × Category × Time × Challenge | 20 XP/day |
| Habits | 13–15 XP each | 10 XP/day |
| Journal | 25–100 XP per entry | 10 XP/day |
| Pomodoro | 13 XP per session | 5 XP/day |
| Weekly Review | 50 XP | Once/week |
| **TOTAL** | — | **50 XP/day** |

### Level Progression

| Levels | XP Required | Title |
|--------|------------|-------|
| 1–10 | 500 XP/level | NOVICE |
| 11–25 | 750 XP/level | APPRENTICE |
| 26–50 | 1,200 XP/level | JOURNEYMAN |
| 51–75 | 1,800 XP/level | EXPERT |
| 76–100 | 2,500 XP/level | LEGEND |

### Achievements (30 total)

| Category | Count | Examples |
|----------|-------|---------|
| Milestones | 14 | First task, 100 tasks, 1000 words written, Level 50 |
| Streaks | 7 | 7-day, 30-day, 100-day, 365-day streaks |
| Skills | 4 | Dawn Operator, Speed Demon, Time Master |
| Hidden | 4 | Midnight Thoughts, Perfectionist, Recovery |

## 🗄 Firestore Schema

11 collections powering the entire app:

```
users/
├── user_stats/          # Level, XP, streaks, achievements, activity log
├── user_settings/       # Preferences, theme, difficulty, unlocked items

content/
├── tasks/               # Tasks with priority, category, XP, 24h countdown
├── journals/            # Journal entries with mood, energy, word count
├── habits/              # Habit definitions with frequency and target streak
├── habit_logs/          # Daily habit completion logs (one per habit per day)
├── time_blocks/         # Timetable blocks (task, event, break, routine)

intelligence/
├── life_snapshots/      # Weekly Wheel of Life radar chart snapshots
├── weekly_reviews/      # Sunday debrief entries
├── coach_messages/      # AI coach conversation history (last 50 messages)
├── motivation_items/    # Motivation portal items (links, quotes, text)
```

## 🔒 Security

- **Firestore rules** — every document requires auth + userId ownership
- **XP anti-cheat** — server-side validation prevents XP injection
- **Field immutability** — userId and createdAt locked after document creation
- **Rate limiting** — max document counts per collection per user
- **Input sanitization** — strips XSS vectors from all text inputs
- **Gemini API key** — server-side only, never exposed to client
